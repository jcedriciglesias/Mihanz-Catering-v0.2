Mihanz Catering

The Mihanz Catering is a food service business that has been serving its client for 8 years and continues, located at 181 Calderon St.Subic Baliuag ,Bulacan, San Rafael, Philippines, 3010. founded in 2015, the business started through Mr. Antonio Javier’s passion for cooking and his business partner Golando Masi saw it as an opportunity. He offered Antonio Javier, which happened to be his brother in law to finance the business. Antonio agreed and the business started as small canteen. As a fellow business minded, Mr. Antonio Javier already set his plans and aiming to make a catering which after years of hard work was finally achieved, the business was named after Mirai Javier “Mi” whom is Antonio’s daughter and Yohanz Masi who is the son of Golando both combined to form the now “Mihanz Catering”
